Visualization of Census 2011

Visualization of the Census 2011 data of the Govt. Of India

About the Project

This visualization is developed by Zaher Abdul Azeez as a part of Summar Project 2015 with Prof. Girish Dalvi at IDC IIT Bombay

This is hopefully the fastest and lightest ever visualization of Census of India 2011

The Project is still in development and hence has scope of Improvement


How to Use

You can select a data set from the dropdown menus on the right or below, depending on your device

The data gets visualised on the map as a heat map - red indicating high & blue indicating low

In order to visualize custom data, use the "Index Tool" at the bottom

You can take ratios, add, subtract, multiply and further calculate, using the tool. Just like a calculator!

For Example: To find the density of literate persons take ratio between Total literate population and Area. ie; Total literate population / Area
